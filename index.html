Our Story

our place is a place where you can enjoy the rich and diverse flavors of Ethiopia in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you are vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, or a meat lover, you will find something to satisfy your taste buds from our menu of traditional dishes. Try our injera, a sourdough flatbread that is used to scoop up stews and salads, or our kitfo, a spicy minced beef dish that is served raw or cooked. You can also sample our coffee, which is roasted and brewed in front of you, or our tej, a honey wine that is fermented with gesho leaves.
