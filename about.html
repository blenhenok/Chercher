About us ☰

Welcome to Ethiopian Authentic Restaurant, where you can enjoy the rich and diverse cuisine of Ethiopia. We are a family-owned and operated restaurant that has been serving the Dire Dawa community since 2020. Our mission is to provide our customers with a memorable dining experience that showcases the culture and flavors of Ethiopia. Ethiopian cuisine is characterized by the use of various spices, herbs, grains, legumes, and meats. It is also known for its unique way of eating, where food is served on a large platter of injera, a sourdough flatbread made from teff flour. Injera serves as both a plate and a utensil, as diners tear off pieces of it and use it to scoop up the stews, salads, and sauces that accompany it. Eating with injera is not only delicious, but also fun and communal. At Ethiopian Authentic Restaurant, we offer a variety of dishes that cater to different tastes and preferences. Whether you are a vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or meat lover, you will find something to satisfy your palate.
