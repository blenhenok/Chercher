About Us


East African Kitchen

  • Our restaurant brings the authentic flavors of Ethiopia to your plate. We pride ourselves on using fresh and high-quality ingredients to give you a true taste of Ethiopian cuisine.Our chefs have years of experience in preparing traditional Ethiopian dishes, including injera, doro wat, and kitfo, as well as vegetarian and vegan options. We carefully select our ingredients to ensure that every dish is rich in flavor.


  • At our restaurant, we want to provide you with an experience that goes beyond just a meal. We want to immerse you in Ethiopian culture, with our vibrant decor and warm hospitality. Whether you're dining with us for the first time or are a regular customer, our staff will make you feel welcomed and comfortable.We also offer catering services, bringing the tastes of Ethiopia to your special events. Whatever the occasion, our team is dedicated to creating an unforgettable culinary experience for you and your guests.
